Project description: XR Shots is a high-performance Digital Asset Management (DAM) system built on a cloud-native Content Engine that automates video transcoding and frame extraction. Its technical core features a metadata-driven relational database designed to map complex industry credits, powered by an Elastic search style engine for rapid discovery across half a million assets.
